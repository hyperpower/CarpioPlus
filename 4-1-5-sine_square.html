2.4.5. Sine Square¶

2.4.5.1. Problem Description¶

The domain is a unit square \(x \in [0.0, 1.0], y \in [0.0, 1.0]\). The boudary conditions are

\[\begin{split}\phi =\sin^2(\frac{10}{3} \pi y) &, \quad \text{if} \quad x = 0.0 \quad \text{and} \quad y = [0.0, 0.3]\\ \frac{d \phi}{d x} = 0 &, \quad \text{if} \quad x = 1.0\\ \phi = 0 &, \quad \text{if} \quad y = 0.0\\ \frac{d \phi}{d y} = 0 &, \quad \text{if} \quad y = 1.0\\\end{split}\]

The velocity is \(\mathbf{u}=(1, 1)\).
