2.4.8. Rotational Two Gates¶

2.4.8.1. Problem Description¶

The domain is a unit square \(x \in [-1.0, 1.0], y \in [0.0, 1.0]\). The boudary conditions are

\[\begin{split}\phi = 1 &, \quad \text{if} \quad y = 0.0 \quad \text{and} \quad y = [-0.8, -0.6] \quad \text{or} \quad y = [-0.4, -0.2]\\ \frac{d \phi}{d x} = 0 &, \quad \text{if} \quad x = 0 \quad \text{and} \quad x = 1\\ \frac{d \phi}{d y} = 0 &, \quad \text{if} \quad y = 0.0 \quad \text{and} \quad x = [0.0, 1.0]\\ \frac{d \phi}{d y} = 0 &, \quad \text{if} \quad y = 1.0 \\\end{split}\]

The velocity is \(\mathbf{u}=(y, -x)\).
