1.2.1. Non-incremental Projection¶

1.2.1.1. Introduction¶

This is the simplest pressure-corrction scheme has originally been proposed by [C1]. Using the implicit Euler time stepping, the algorithm is as follows: Set \(u^0 = u_0\), then for \(k \ge 0\) compute \((\hat{u}^{k+1}, u^{k+1}, p^{k+1})\) by solving

\[\frac{(\tilde{u} - u^{k})}{\Delta t} + u^k \cdot (\nabla u^k) = \frac{1}{Re} \nabla^2 \tilde{u}\]
\[\frac{(u^{k+1} - \tilde{u})}{\Delta t} + u^k \cdot (\nabla u^k) = \frac{1}{Re} \nabla^2 \tilde{u}\]

Take the divergence operator on both side of Eq.

\[\begin{split}\frac{1}{\Delta t}(\nabla \cdot u^{k+1} - \nabla \cdot \tilde{u}) &= -\nabla \cdot \nabla p^{k+1}, \\ - \frac{1}{\Delta t} \nabla \cdot \tilde{u} &= -\nabla \cdot \nabla p^{k+1}, \\ \frac{1}{\Delta t} \nabla \cdot \tilde{u} &= \nabla \cdot \nabla p^{k+1}\end{split}\]

Solve above equation, \(p^{k+1}\) can be obtained. Then,

\[u^{k+1} = \tilde{u} - \Delta t \nabla p^{k+1}\]

1.2.1.2. Calculation of an Auxiliary Veloticy Field¶

In order to calculate an auxiliary velocity \(\tilde{u}\), we follow the scheme in [C2]. The scheme is one of the splitting and fractional-step methods, which is a variant of the ADI method.

\[\frac{(u^* - u^{k})}{\Delta t} + u^k (\frac{\partial u^k}{\partial x}) = \frac{1}{Re} \frac{\partial^2 u^*}{\partial x^{2}}\]
\[\frac{(u^{**} - u^{*})}{\Delta t} + u^* (\frac{\partial u^*}{\partial y}) = \frac{1}{Re} \frac{\partial^2 u^{**}}{\partial y^2}\]
\[\frac{(\tilde{u} - u^{**})}{\Delta t} + u^{**} (\frac{\partial u^{**}}{\partial z}) = \frac{1}{Re} \frac{\partial^2 \tilde{u}}{\partial z^2}\]

Note that Euquations are of implicit form for each intermediate field. These schems are unconditionally stable, but total stability condition is not strictly derived.

1.2.1.2.1. Boundary condition for \(\tilde{u}\)¶

Since,

\[\tilde{u} = u^{k + 1} + \Delta t \nabla p^n\]

For stationary wall \(u^{k + 1} = 0\), Then,

\[\tilde{u} = \Delta t \nabla p^n\]

For the direction normal to the wall, say \(x\),

\[\tilde{u} = \frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} (P_g^n - P_b^n)\]

where, \(P_g^n\) is ghost node, \(P_b^n\) is boundary node.
