1.2.2. Non-incremental projection method¶

The usual non-incremental algorithm is proposed by Chorin. The definition of the parameters are shown as follows:

Parameters

Illustion

Time interval

\([0, T]\)

Time subinterval

\(N\)

Time step

\(\Delta t = T / N\)

Velocities

\({\tilde{\mathbf{u}}^{n+1}}_{n = 0,...,N}\)

Velocities

\({\mathbf{u}^{n+1}}_{n = 0,...,N}\)

Pressure

\({\mathbf{p}^{n+1}}_{n = 0,...,N}\)

The governing equations are:

  • Momentum equation:

\[\frac{\rho}{\Delta t}(\tilde{\mathbf{u}}^{n+1} - \mathbf{u}^{n+1}) - \mu \Delta \tilde{\mathbf{u}}^{n+1} = \mathbf{u}^{n+1}\]
