1.1.2.1. Intorduction¶

The ENO (essentially non-oscilatory) schemes approximating systems of hyperbolic conservation laws of the type:

\[\begin{split}\mathbf{\phi} + \nabla \cdot f(\mathbf{\phi}) &= 0 \\ \mathbf{u}(\mathbf{x}, 0) &= \mathbf{u}^0(\mathbf{x})\end{split}\]

1.1.2.2. ENO-Roe¶

algotithm

Step 1 Compute the divided difference table of \(f\), and identify

\[H[x_{l-1/2}, x_{l+1/2}] = f[u(x_l)]\]
\[H[x_{l-1/2}, x_{l+1/2}, ... , x_{l+k+1/2}] = \frac{1}{1 + k} f[u(x_l), ..., u(x_{l+k})]\]
\[k = 1, 2, ... , r\]

if \(k = 1\)

\[\begin{split}H[x_{l-1/2}, x_{l+1/2}, x_{l+1+1/2}] &= \frac{1}{1 + 1} f[u(x_l), u(x_{l + 1})] \\ H[x_{l-1/2}, x_{l+1/2}, x_{l+3/2}] &= \frac{1}{2} f[u(x_l), u(x_{l + 1})] \\ &= \frac{1}{2} \frac{u(x_{l + 1}) - u(x_l)}{x_{l + 1} - x_l}\end{split}\]

if \(k = 2\)

\[\begin{split}H[x_{l-1/2}, x_{l+1/2}, x_{l+1+1/2}, x_{l+2+1/2}] &= \frac{1}{1 + 2} f[u(x_l), u(x_{l + 1}), u(x_{l+2})] \\ H[x_{l-1/2}, x_{l+1/2}, x_{l+3/2}, x_{l+5+1/2}] &= \frac{1}{3} f[u(x_l), u(x_{l + 1})] \\ &= \frac{1}{3} \frac{f[u(x_{l + 1}),u(x_{l + 2})] - f[u(x_{l}),u(x_{l+1})]}{x_{l+2} - x_l}\end{split}\]
