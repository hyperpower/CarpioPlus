2.4.2. Gate 1D¶

2.4.2.1. Problem Description¶

The 1D gate profile has been initialized as:

(2.7)¶\[\begin{split}x &\in [0.25, 0.75] , \phi = 1, \\ x &\notin [0.25, 0.75], \phi = 0\end{split}\]

The velocity is \(u=1\) m/s. After 1 seconds (100 steps with \(\Delta t = 0.01\)), the final profile should be

(2.8)¶\[\begin{split}x \in [1.25, 1.75]&, \phi = 1, \\ else &, \phi = 0\end{split}\]
